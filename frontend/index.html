👋 Welcome to LandcareLink

This page is a directory of community environmental groups. You can browse them on the map, search and filter the list, and add or edit group records right in the table.

  • Add a group — fill in the highlighted row at the top of the table and click Add.
  • Edit a group — click Edit on any row to change its details inline, then Save.
  • Use the map — each coloured dot is a group; click a dot to see its name, type, region, and contact email.
  • Search & filter — narrow the list by name, region, or email, or filter by type and region. Use Clear filters to reset.
